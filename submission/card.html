Tiger

Tigers are known to save left overs of prey that is too large to consume by hiding it under leaves.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 2 - 3.9m (Gender a factor)
  • Average Lifespan: 20-26 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands,swamps,tall grassy locations

Tigers are the largest cat species existing in the wild. Descended from the sabre tooth tiger, they rely on their canines to hunt and are some of the deadliest predators to exist today.
